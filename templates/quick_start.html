AI Art System - Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Upload Your Audio Clip

  • Click on the “Upload Audio” button.
  • Select an audio file from your device (supported formats: .mp3, .wav).
  • Click “Analyze Audio” to process your clip.

Step 2: Configure Your Segments

  • Each segment should be around 5 seconds, with 1 second for transitions.
  • If a segment is shorter than 3 seconds, consider removing transitions to avoid overshadowing.
  • Suggested Ending Effects: Zoom in, Zoom out.
  • Suggested Transitions:
    • Pan Up, Pan Down
    • Pan Left, Pan Right
  • Quick Setup Tip: Copy and paste the same settings across all segments for a fast trial run.

Step 3: Analyze and Generate

  • Click “Generate Art” to process the audio and create visuals.
  • Preview the output and make adjustments if needed.

Step 4: Export or Share

  • Download your creation by clicking “Export”.
  • Share it on social platforms or with collaborators.
Additional Tips:
Experiment with different transition effects and segment lengths to enhance creativity.
